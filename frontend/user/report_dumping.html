Reporting Guidelines

What to Report
  • Large piles of trash in public areas
  • Hazardous waste dumped in unauthorized locations
  • Construction debris left on streets or sidewalks
  • Abandoned furniture or appliances
  • Illegal dumping in parks or natural areas
What Not to Report
  • Normal household waste in designated bins
  • Overflowing bins that are scheduled for pickup
  • Minor litter (use the city's litter reporting system)
  • Issues with collection timing (contact customer service)
How to Locate
  • Use Google Maps to get exact coordinates
  • Note nearby landmarks or street names
  • Take clear photos showing the location
  • Include date and time in your description
Follow-Up

You'll receive a confirmation email with your report number. We'll contact you if we need additional information.
