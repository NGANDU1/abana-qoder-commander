Recycling Tips & Education

Paper & Cardboard

  • Remove any plastic wrapping
  • Flatten boxes to save space
  • Keep paper dry and clean
  • No greasy pizza boxes

Plastic & Glass

  • Rinse containers before recycling
  • Remove lids and caps
  • Don't mix different plastic types
  • Check recycling numbers

Organic Waste

  • Use compostable bags only
  • Include fruit and vegetable scraps
  • No meat or dairy products
  • Keep bin covered and clean

Hazardous Waste

  • Never mix with regular waste
  • Use designated drop-off points
  • Keep in original containers
  • Label clearly if repackaged
